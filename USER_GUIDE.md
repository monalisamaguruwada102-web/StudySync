# StudySync User Guide

Welcome to **StudySync** - Your Premium Study Management System!

## Getting Started

### 1. Login
- Open the app and select your account from the login screen
- Enter your password to access your dashboard
- Only authorized accounts can access the system

### 2. Dashboard Overview
After logging in, you'll see your main dashboard with:
- **Study Hours**: Total time you've logged studying
- **Active Modules**: Your enrolled courses/subjects
- **Pending Tasks**: Upcoming assignments and deadlines
- **Study Streak**: Consecutive days of study activity
- **Weekly Trend Chart**: Visual of your study patterns
- **Module Distribution**: How your time is split across subjects
